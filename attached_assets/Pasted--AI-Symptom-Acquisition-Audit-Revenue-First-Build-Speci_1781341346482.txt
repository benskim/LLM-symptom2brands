# AI Symptom Acquisition Audit

## Revenue-First Build Specification v2

### PRIMARY OBJECTIVE

This project does NOT exist to build software.

This project exists to close the first 3 customers.

All engineering decisions must be evaluated against one question:

"Will this help generate a paid audit within the next 30 days?"

If not, do not build it.

---

# BUSINESS CONTEXT

Operator:

* Solo founder
* Korea based
* No sales team
* No designer
* No DevOps

Target Market:

* US Shopify Gut Health Brands
* Probiotic Brands
* Digestive Health DTC

Customer Size:

* 5–20 employees
* Founder-led growth
* Shopify storefront
* Subscription products preferred

Target Outcome:

* First customer within 30 days
* First $300 revenue within 100 days

---

# PRODUCT DEFINITION

Customer does not buy software.

Customer buys:

"Why is ChatGPT recommending competitors instead of us?"

The deliverable is a report.

The software exists only to generate the report faster.

---

# REQUIRED OUTPUT

For every brand audit generate:

## Section 1

Visibility Score

Questions:

* Is the brand recommended?
* How often?

Output:

* mention count
* visibility score

---

## Section 2

Competitor Capture

Questions:

* Which brands appear instead?

Output:

* ranked competitor list
* frequency

---

## Section 3

Citation Surface

Questions:

* Which websites are AI models citing?

Output:

* top domains
* citation counts

---

## Section 4

Evidence Gap

Questions:

* What signals appear missing?

Output:

* evidence-backed observations only

Allowed language:

"Possible gap"

Forbidden language:

"Root cause"

"Definitive reason"

"Guaranteed explanation"

---

## Section 5

Quick Wins

Generate:

* FAQ ideas
* symptom pages
* trust signals
* citation acquisition opportunities

Maximum:

10 recommendations

Prioritize highest leverage actions.

---

# MVP ARCHITECTURE

Build only four modules.

Module A

Visibility Sampling

Requirements:

* OpenAI only
* 5 samples per prompt
* sequential execution
* retry 3 times

No async.

No queue.

No orchestration framework.

---

Module B

Citation Extraction

Priority order:

1. structured citations
2. fallback regex

Do not build complex parsers.

---

Module C

Competitor Aggregation

Extract:

* brand names
* mention counts

Simple frequency table.

No entity resolution engine.

No knowledge graph.

---

Module D

Evidence Gap Generator

Single model call.

Temperature:

0

Input:

* visibility data
* citation data
* competitor data

Output:

structured JSON

No autonomous agents.

No multi-step reasoning chains.

No LangGraph.

No CrewAI.

No LangChain agents.

---
